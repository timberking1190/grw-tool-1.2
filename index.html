The Scientific Foundation

Six frameworks.
One unified instrument.

This is not a proprietary algorithm. Every signal category traces to decades of peer-reviewed research across four continents. We built the first instrument to synthesise these frameworks into a single, real-time intelligence system. We cite the science because the science is the product.

01
FACS
Facial Action Coding System
Dr. Paul Ekman and Dr. Wallace Friesen spent 15 years cataloguing every discrete movement the human face can make. The result — 44 Action Units, each mapped to specific muscle contractions — became the gold standard in clinical psychology, forensic interviewing, and behavioural medicine. This system reads all 44, per frame, in your footage.
Ekman & Friesen · 1978 · University of California San Francisco
02
KINESICS
Birdwhistell's Kinesics
Ray Birdwhistell spent three decades studying body movement as a structured communication system. His taxonomy of 120+ postural and gestural signal classes — the nonverbal vocabulary operating below conscious awareness — is the framework behind elite forensic interviewing, clinical diagnosis, and performance coaching that reads more than words.
Birdwhistell · 1970 · University of Pennsylvania
03
FLOW STATE
Csikszentmihalyi's Flow Research
Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi spent 30 years documenting optimal human performance across athletes, surgeons, chess masters, and artists. The physiological markers of flow state — blink rate variance, gaze behaviour, postural tension patterns — appear consistently across every domain. This system reads those markers directly from footage.
Csikszentmihalyi · 1990 · University of Chicago
04
SYNCHRONY
Interpersonal Synchrony
Daniël Lakens' research on behavioural synchrony quantified what elite coaches have always sensed: when people align in posture, gesture timing, and micro-expression patterns, trust, cohesion, and collective performance follow. This is the science behind Group Dynamics analysis — the system maps it across every visible subject in the frame.
Lakens · 2010 · Eindhoven University of Technology
05
HOFSTEDE
Hofstede's 6-Dimension Cultural Model
Geert Hofstede's 40-year study across 76 countries produced the definitive model for how cultural psychology shapes individual behaviour under pressure. Power Distance, Uncertainty Avoidance, Individualism, Masculinity, Long-Term Orientation, Indulgence — each dimension directly conditions how performance signals manifest and how coaching interventions land.
Hofstede · 1980–2010 · University of Maastricht
06
COGNITIVE LOAD
Sweller's Cognitive Load Theory
John Sweller's foundational work on working memory and cognitive overload explains why elite performers break down under novel pressure. High cognitive load leaves measurable traces in eye movement, micro-expression timing, and postural behaviour — traces that precede performance breakdown by seconds to minutes. The system reads them before the scoreboard does.
Sweller · 1988 · University of New South Wales

Common Questions

The questions that
block the buy.

Coaches and executives ask us the same things before subscribing. Here are straight answers — for both sport and business applications.

Does my video ever leave my device? ▾
No — and this is by architecture, not just policy. The entire analysis pipeline runs inside your browser. Your video is never uploaded, transmitted, or received by any server we operate. When you close the tab, the footage is gone. This is the reason we can guarantee it with confidence: we have no technical ability to receive your video.
What sports and industries does this work for? ▾
The six frameworks this system is built on — Ekman's FACS, Birdwhistell's Kinesics, Csikszentmihalyi's flow research, and three others — are domain-agnostic. They measure behavioural signals that appear consistently across elite sport, executive leadership, clinical settings, and high-stakes business environments. Current users include team sport coaches, individual performance coaches, HR directors, executive coaches, and sport psychologists. The system works with any footage where a person is clearly visible.
Is this HIPAA or GDPR compliant? ▾
PIPEDA and GDPR compliant by design. Because no video data is transmitted or stored, the primary data privacy risk is eliminated at the architecture level. We do not capture biometric identifiers — the system measures Action Units and postural patterns, not identity. Your account data (email, subscription) is encrypted in transit and at rest. If you are working in a clinical or institutional context and need a Data Processing Agreement, contact us at support@boostinnovation.ca.
What's the difference between Standard and Detailed? ▾
Standard gives you the complete facial expression analysis (44 Action Units), body language classification (120+ signal classes), performance timeline, and a four-perspective coaching brief — all in a branded PDF. This is everything most coaches and HR professionals need for day-to-day use.

Detailed adds Mental Focus and Flow State analysis (Csikszentmihalyi's framework applied to cognitive load markers in your footage), plus deeper coaching calibration. If you're working with elite athletes, executives in high-stakes situations, or doing academic research, Detailed gives you the cognitive layer that Standard doesn't.
How accurate is the analysis? ▾
The system applies the same peer-reviewed frameworks used in clinical psychology, forensic interviewing, and elite coaching programmes. Accuracy is directly correlated to footage quality — clear lighting, stable camera angle, and the subject visible from the chest up produces the most precise signal classification. The report always includes a confidence indicator and never extrapolates beyond what the footage supports. We cite the science behind every signal category so you can assess the methodology yourself.
Can I analyse group or team footage? ▾
The current system analyses one primary subject per analysis run. For team or group footage, focus the frame on one individual at a time, or use the Group Culture Psychology layer (available in the Detailed and Deep Dive tiers) which applies Hofstede's cultural dimension framework to collective behavioural patterns when multiple individuals are visible. Run the analysis multiple times for different subjects — each analysis is separate and your subscription covers unlimited runs.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes — no contracts, no lock-in, no questions asked. Cancel from your subscriber dashboard at any time. Your subscription stays active through the end of the current billing period. We don't do retention tactics. If the tool isn't delivering value for your coaching or business practice, cancel and you owe nothing further.
Is this tool suitable for recruiting or hiring decisions? ▾
This system is built for performance coaching and professional development — not hiring decisions. Using behavioural analysis as a primary input to employment decisions raises significant ethical and legal considerations in most jurisdictions. The appropriate use cases are: coaching preparation, leadership development, communication coaching, interview practice (candidate self-review), and post-performance debrief. If you are an HR professional using this tool, we recommend it for development programmes, not selection processes.
